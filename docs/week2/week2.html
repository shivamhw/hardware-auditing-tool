Objective

Experiment with SPI Flash and add the functionality of reading and downloading the firmware from IoT device.

Justification

Most of the electronic devices like routers, IoT device, multimedia player or even fridge and washing machine needs a chip to store the firmware so to say. Generally in common devices the firmware is stored separately, ie. not in the main memory of microcontroller or processor. This is to ensure that firmware could be updated safely and also because of price factor. The preffered choice to store firmware is a additional chip known as flash (there are many type and protocol). They are much easy to target.
The type we are targetting is SPI flash which is very common in routers and motherboard bios chips.

Steps :

  1. aquire parts and target device (Done)
  2. perform firmware extraction on at least 5 device to gather insights (2 devices done)
